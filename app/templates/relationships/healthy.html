Relationships

What does a healthy relationship look like?

How do you know if your current relationships are healthy? We all have multiple relationships. Some folks we see seldom and so the quality of the relationship is not so vital. However, if we are talking about a relationship that has a daily impact on us, it is important to determine if it is beneficial to us.

When you are in a good relationship:

-you look forward to spending time with that person

-you feel free to be yourself when you are with them

-you fell the relationships is mutually beneficial.

-You need to give and receive equally. It is understood, that at times there will be an unbalance in the give and take, especially during times when one person is under extra pressure or stress. This will be the case from time to time while you are in school. Your friend/ spouse/parent/child needs to understand this and step up to the plate when you need them to without trying to make you feel guilty.
