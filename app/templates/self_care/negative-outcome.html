Self Care

What self care means is different for everyone. The most important thing to remember is "everything in moderation". A college student has several competing interests and it can be tough to juggle everything, ask for help when you need it and make sure to carve time out for yourself. Even if you have become amazing at multitasking, you may find that you have less energy and are becoming overwhelmed simply because you are constantly doing so much all at once. It can be disappointing to burn-out, so tackle your challenges one at a time and allow yourself the time you need to relax and refocus your energy.
