Self Care

Don't Panic!

If it is mid semester and you are starting to feel in over your head, don't panic. It's never too late to start.

- Seek out some help from a counsellor or student success specialist.

- Figure out exactly where you stand in your classes.

- Figure out how you can manage the workload.

- Most of all don't be too hard on yourself, think positively about what you have been able to accomplish, and reward yourself appropriately when you finish a task.
