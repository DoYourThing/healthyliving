Cultural Adjustment

Establishing a Support System

Just because there may no longer be the luxury of a home cooked meal does not mean you can't maintain a similar and healthy diet.

Try and plan your meals in advance, this will help avoid falling back on fast food as a quick and convenient option. Inquire about the College meal plan. This may or may not be the right option for every student.

By familiarizing yourself with the options you will be able to make informed decisions

A new city, school or environment does not necessarily mean you cannot maintain your activity level.

Be creative, learn how to time manage and be open to new options. Visit the college website for information on sports activities, teams, and the gym.
