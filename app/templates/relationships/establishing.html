Relationships

Establishing new relationships

- After class ask some classmates to head over to the cafeteria for a meal or a coffee with you. Getting to know each other outside of the classroom setting is a good way to establish a friendship and everyone likes to eat!

- If you're living in residence or with roommates, keep the door to your room open, this way it signals to others that you are welcoming conversation.

- Join a social club, attend some college events, this will provide you with more opportunities to meet people.

- Don't worry about being shy - in the beginning, everyone is new to the college and trying to make new friends. Be yourself, and don't be afraid to get to know others.
